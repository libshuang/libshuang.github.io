About me

Truth, honesty, respect, and diligence. These are the four ideals that I have rolled with my entire life.

As a first generation immigrant from China, I would tell you about my family and our struggles in America. Not going to lie, it was hard. Its your typical “nothing to something” story. The one thing that bothered me growing up is that there’s so much “noise”. So much noise in everywhere I go. And I understand growing up, in the state of capitalism, where your survival is built on your value to society to survive, that the truth must be bend in hundreds of different variations, to be fed to different types of people, to feed your family and keep a roof under your roof.

This is why I chose my profession as a Data Scientist/Analyst – and/or – Problem Solver. This field is a dream field; everything I asked for. Being paid to spell out the truth through numbers. Embracing my inner curiosity to seek out the objective truth and present findings.
